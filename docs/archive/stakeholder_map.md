# RCM Stakeholder Narrative & Map

**CFO:**
- Cares about cash leakage, payer yield gap trends, forecastable recovery, and risk exposure (overpay/recoupment).

**Revenue Cycle Director:**
- Focuses on denial drivers, workqueue throughput, claim aging, and preventability of denials.

**Billing/Follow-up Manager:**
- Needs a “Top 200 today” actionable list with clear reasons and next steps for each claim.

**Compliance:**
- Monitors CLIA and ownership denial codes, ensuring audit trails and regulatory adherence.

**Ops Analyst:**
- Relies on QC gates and drift monitors to ensure metrics are accurate and trustworthy.

This stakeholder map sets up the context for your decision memo and supports interview storytelling.
